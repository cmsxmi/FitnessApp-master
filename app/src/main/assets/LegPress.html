Leg Press

1) There are pictures above that show you how you should perform the exercise.

2) Sit on the machine with your back and head against the padded support.

3) Place your feet on the footplate about hip-width apart, ensuring that your heels are flat. Your legs should form an angle of about 90 degrees.

4) Using the handles unlock the weights and bring your legs towards you and then push them away from you.
