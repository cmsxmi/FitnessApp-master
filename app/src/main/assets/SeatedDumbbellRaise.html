Seated Side Lateral Dumbbell Raise

1) There are pictures above that show you how you should perform the exercise.

2) Sit at the end of a flat bench with your feet firmly on the floor. Hold the dumbbells with your palms facing in and your arms straight down at your sides at arms' length.

3) Keeping you arms straight start to extend the arms away from your body.

4) Lower the dumbbells back down slowly to the starting position as you inhale.
