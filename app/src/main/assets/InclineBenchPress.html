Barbell Incline Bench Press

1) There are pictures above that show you how you should perform the exercise.

2) Position your body on an incline bench on a 30-45 degree angle.

3) Grab a barbell with an overhand grip that's shoulder-width apart and hold it above your chest.

4) o Extend arms upward, locking out elbows. Bring it down to your chest and then push it back to start position.
