Seated Dumbbell Press

1) There are pictures above that show you how you should perform the exercise.

2) While holding a dumbbell in each hand, sit on a military press bench or utility bench that has back support.

3) Place the dumbbells upright on top of your thighs. Now raise the dumbbells to shoulder height one at a time using your thighs to help propel them up into position.

4) Push the dumbbells above your head and then bring it back down start position.
