Standing Bicep Curl with Dumbbells

1) There are pictures above that show you how you should perform the exercise.

2) Stand up straight with a dumbbell in each hand at arm's length.

3) Keep your elbows close to your torso and rotate the palms of your hands until they are facing forward.

4) Now, keeping the upper arms stationary, exhale and curl the weights while contracting your biceps.
