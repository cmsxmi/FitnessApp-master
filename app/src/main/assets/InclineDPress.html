Incline Dumbell Press

1) There are pictures above that show you how you should perform the exercise.

2) Lie back on an incline bench with a dumbbell in each hand atop your thighs.

3) Then, using your thighs to help push the dumbbells up, lift the dumbbells one at a time so that you can hold them at shoulder width.

4) Bring the dumbbell down towards your chest and push back to start position.
