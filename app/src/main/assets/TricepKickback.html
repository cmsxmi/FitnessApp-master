Triceps Kickback with Dumbbells

1) There are pictures above that show you how you should perform the exercise.

2) Start with a dumbbell in each hand and your palms facing your torso. Keep your back straight with a slight bend in the knees.

3) Now, while keeping your upper arms stationary, exhale and use your triceps to lift the weights until the arm is fully extended.

4) After a brief pause at the top contraction, inhale and slowly lower the dumbbells back down.
