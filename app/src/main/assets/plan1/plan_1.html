EXCERSISE: FLAT BENCHPRESS

SETS: 10 X 3

1) Position your body on a flat bench

2) Grab a bar in each hand that's shoulder-width apart and hold it above your chest

3) Lower the bar straight down in a slow, controlled movement to your chest

4) Pause, then press the bar in a straight line back up to the starting position

EXCERSISE: WIDE GRIP LAT PULL DOWN

SETS: 8 X 12

1) Sit at a lat pulldown station and grab the bar with an overhand wide grip

2) Your arms should be completely straight and your back should be upright

3) Pull your shoulder blades down and back, and bring the bar to your chest

4) Pause, then slowly return to the starting position

EXCERSISE: LEG PRESS

SETS: 3 X 8

1) Slowly push the platform, don't lock the knees

2) Bring back the platform to the staring position

ABS CRUNCHES

SETS: 1 X 8

1) Lie flat on your back with knees bent 90 degrees

2) Hands on the side of the head

3) Push your lower back on floor when you try to lift the shoulders
