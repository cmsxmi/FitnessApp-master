EXCERSISE: MOUNTAIN CLIMBERS

SETS: 2 X 8

1) Get into a push up position

2) With your back arched

3) Then bring the right knee towards the chest followed by the left

4) Return to the normal position

EXCERSISE: SQUATS

SETS: 8 - 12 X 5

1) Stand holding a light barbell under your neck

2) Squat back and down until the top of your thighs are parallel to the floor

3) Push from your hips back to a standing position

EXCERSISE:INCLINE BENCH PRESS

SETS: 4 X 8

1) Position your body on an incline bench on a 30-degree angle

2) Grab a dumbbell in each hand that's shoulder-width apart and hold it above your chest

3) Lower the dumbbell straight down in a slow, controlled movement to your chest

4) Pause, then press the dumbbell in a straight line back up to the starting position

EXCERSISE: SHOULDER PRESS

SETS: 2 X 8

1) Hold a pair of dumbbells just outside of your shoulders

2) Make sure arms are bent and palms facing each other

3) Press both dumbbells up until the weights are overhead and your arms are straight

4) Pause, then lower the dumbbells under control back to the starting position

EXCERSISE: TRICEPS CABLE ROPE

SETS: 3 X 12

1) Grab the rope with a shoulder-width

2) Bring your elbows to your sides so your forearms are parallel to the floor

3) Extend your forearms straight down by contracting the triceps

4) When your arms are fully extended

5) Pause, then reverse to return to the start position
