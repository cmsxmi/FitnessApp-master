EXCERSISE: LAT PULL DOWN

SETS: 3 X 8

1) Sit at a lat pulldown station and grab the bar with an overhand grip

2) Your arms should be completely straight and your back should be upright

3) Pull your shoulder blades down and back, and bring the bar to your chest

4) Pause, then slowly return to the starting position

EXCERSISE: SQUATS

SETS: 8 - 12 X 5

1) Stand holding a light barbell under your neck

2) Squat back and down until the top of your thighs are parallel to the floor

3) Push from your hips back to a standing position

EXCERSISE: BARBELL ROWS

SETS: 3 X 8

1) Hold the barbell with an overhand grip, slightly wider that your shoulder width apart

2) Legs slightly bent with back straight and chest up

3) Row the weight upwards towards the chest

4) Pause, then slowly return to the starting position

EXCERSISE: FACE PULL SHOULDERS

SETS: 2 X 8

1) Grab a rope on the cable, take few steps back to get the full stretch

2) Pull towards your forehead

4) Pause, then slowly return to the starting position

EXCERSISE: PLANK

DURATION: 4 X 50 SECONDS

1) Plant the hands directly under the shoulders

2) Ground the toes into the floor and squeeze the glutes to stabilize the body

4) Neutralize the neck and spine by looking at a spot on the floor with the head in line with the back

EXCERSISE: SHOULDERS LATRAL RAISERS

SETS: 3 X 8

1) Pick a dumbbell and stand with them by your sides. Palms facing the body

2) Lift the dumbbells side with slight bend at your elbows

3) Lift until your arms are parallel to the floor

4) Slowly return to the starting position

EXCERSISE: PULL UPS

SETS: 2 X 8

1) Grab the pull up bar with overhand grip and grip narrower than shoulder width

2) Pull yourself up until chin is above the bar

3) Lift until your arms are parallel to the floor

4) Pause, slowly lower back to starting position
